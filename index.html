Home Made Food – Both Taste and Love

Hello, Welcome to Sameer - the Chef, a place dedicated to people who love to cook home made food. Home made food is not only delicious, quality controlled and healthy, but also, a way to express yourself to your loved ones. I hope you find these recepies tasty and healthy !!!.

Home Made Recepies

Food prepared at home is always delicious. Why? Because at home, it is customised with your own taste, with added love and emotions. We, through this page really want to encourage people to cook tasty restaurant like food at home.

Easy to cook

Most of our recepies here are easy to cook and with easily available ingredients in your nearest super market. The step by step guide to each recepie along with the quantity and some extra cooking tips will surely by loved by you.

Health and Taste

Health is an important factor for us while preparing any recepie for you to cook. In our recepies, we try to include healthy food with the blend of taste in order to keep the balance towards both. We are looking forward to include a seperate section for health food recepies for our fitness folks.
